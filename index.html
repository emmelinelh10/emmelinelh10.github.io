Computational Modelling of Motion Perception
How does the brain get fooled into seeing objects speed up or slow down when moving at a constant speed? This research models a biologically-inspired, insect-derived motion detector in MATLAB — showing illusory animation emerges naturally from the algorithm's temporal filtering properties. First author publication in Proceedings of the Royal Society B.
