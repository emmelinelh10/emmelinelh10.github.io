Projects

Computational Modelling of Motion Perception Illusions

How does the brain get fooled into seeing objects speed up or slow down when they're moving at a constant speed? This research explores that question by modelling a biologically-inspired, insect-derived motion detector in MATLAB — showing that illusory animation emerges naturally from the algorithm's temporal filtering properties. Findings were published in Proceedings of the Royal Society B.
